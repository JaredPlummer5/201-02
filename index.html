Self Talk

Positive self-talk involves speaking to oneself in a kind, supportive, and encouraging manner. This type of self-talk can help boost self-confidence, improve motivation, and promote a more positive outlook on life. Negative self-talk, on the other hand, involves speaking to oneself in a critical, judgmental, and harsh manner. This type of self-talk can lead to feelings of anxiety, depression, and low self-esteem. If you find that your self-talk tends to be negative, it can be helpful to practice mindfulness and self-compassion techniques to shift your mindset towards a more positive and supportive approach.
